STOLEN VOICES

By Do Hyun Nam & Santiago Jiménez

Stolen Voices is a non-linear story game where the user has to make a series of decisions that lead to certain outcomes. Its main goal is to make the user reflect on all the gender-related violence happening in the country and to create awareness that in such situations, the decision the victim makes if no influential factor to justify the actions of the perpetrators. At the end of the story, you'll be presented a real situation that happened to someone or a factual data about the situation, just because she made the same decisions you've made in the game. It is impossible for us to fully understand the pain and frustrations them and their families have gone through, but we think it is possible to grow empathy and solidarity of what's been happening in our society. To raise our voices, to help out fight unfairness and injustice starting now, at last you'll be presented on what you can do to prevent and eradicate violence and unfairness. 


 The stories presented at the end of each branch are real life events, as well as the names presented. It is of great importance to remember their names, to humanize them and not let their memories and sufferings fade away as just another case of femicide, society has the responsibility to remember their lives, their dreams, their work, and most importantly their suffering.

The storylines presented on the game are solely fictitious, and any resemblance to a real life situation is merely coincidental.
